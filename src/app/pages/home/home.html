Why OpenAir?

Built by podcast lovers, for podcast lovers. Every feature designed with you in mind.

Subscribe to Any RSS Feed

OpenAir supports all podcast RSS feeds. Find your favorite shows or discover new ones through OPML import.

Ad-Free Listening

The app itself contains no ads. If publishers include ads in their media, 100% of the revenue goes to them.

Listen Anywhere

Download episodes for offline listening on any supported platform. Auto-download keeps your queue fresh.

Built with Love

OpenAir is developed by volunteers who care about podcasting. No corporate interest, just community-driven development.

Chapter Support

Navigate through podcast chapters with ease. Skip to the sections that matter most to you.

Open Dashboard

Connect to podcasters via other platforms and see what's happening in your favorite shows.
