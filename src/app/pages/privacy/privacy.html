Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. Here's how OpenAir protects it.

Our Commitment to Privacy

OpenAir is built with privacy as a core principle. We do not collect, store, or share any personal data. The app does not include analytics, tracking, or telemetry of any kind.

No Data Collection

OpenAir does not collect any information about you, your device, or your listening habits. There are no analytics SDKs, no tracking pixels, and no data collection services integrated into the app.

Podcast Feeds

When you subscribe to a podcast, OpenAir fetches the RSS feed directly from the podcast publisher's server. This means the podcast publisher may see your IP address, just like when you visit any website. OpenAir has no control over how podcast publishers handle your data.

Third-Party Services

OpenAir uses the PodcastIndex and fyyd APIs for podcast search and discovery. These services receive only the search terms you enter and your IP address, which is inherent to any internet connection. OpenAir does not send any personal data to third-party services.

Open Source

Because OpenAir is fully open source, anyone can inspect the code to verify our privacy claims. The entire source code is available on GitHub.

Updates

This privacy policy may be updated occasionally. We will notify users of any material changes through the app and our blog.
