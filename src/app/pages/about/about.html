About OpenAir

Learn about the project, our mission, and the team behind OpenAir.

Our Mission

OpenAir was created to provide a podcast listening experience that respects your privacy and freedom. We believe that listening to podcasts should be a private, uninterrupted experience without tracking or advertisements.

As an open-source project, OpenAir is built transparently by a community of volunteers. Anyone can inspect the code, suggest improvements, or contribute to make the app better for everyone.

Key Principles

  • Privacy First - No tracking, no data collection, no analytics. Your listening habits are yours alone.
  • Open Source - The entire codebase is available on GitHub for anyone to inspect, modify, and share.
  • Community Driven - Development decisions are made by the community, for the community.
  • No Commercial Interest - OpenAir is built by volunteers without corporate backing or advertising.

Technology

OpenAir is built with Flutter, Google's UI toolkit for building natively compiled applications for mobile, desktop, and web from a single codebase. This allows us to provide a consistent experience across all platforms while maintaining native performance.
